# Scout Sleuth — College Recruiting Intelligence Platform

A real-time recruiting intelligence platform for student-athletes, parents, and high school coaches. Surfaces commitment data and strategic recommendations to address the information asymmetry in college recruiting.

> "Solve a painful problem for a niche audience. Everything else is noise." — JKI Playbook

## Status

- **Phase:** validation
- **Last update:** 2026-06-09
- **Next milestone:** 100 survey responses

## Goal

Help student-athletes and parents make informed recruiting decisions with real-time commitment data, rather than relying on stale information from existing recruiting services.

**Target users:**
- Primary: parents of student-athletes (decision-makers, budget holders)
- Secondary: student-athletes (15–18)
- Tertiary: high school coaches, recruiting consultants
